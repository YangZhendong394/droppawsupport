Pet Health Tracker · Support

📋 About This App

DropPaw is a fully offline, zero‑network pet health logging tool. All data stays exclusively on your device — no account required, no data uploads, no tracking.

Every record you keep (weight, diet, symptoms, etc.) is used only to generate health trends and reports, helping you better understand your pet's well‑being.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I export a health report?

Scroll down on the "Statistics" tab and tap the "Health Report (PDF)" button. The report includes pet profile, weekly trends, symptom summary, and expense breakdown — ready to share with your vet.

Can I track multiple pets?

Yes. Go to Settings → Manage Pets, then tap "Add Pet". After adding, a pet selector will appear at the top of the Home and Statistics screens — tap to switch between pets.

Will I lose my data? How to back up?

All data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete your data (no cloud backup). We recommend exporting PDF reports regularly or taking screenshots as a backup.

Does the app require network access?

No. DropPaw does not request any network permissions. All operations run locally — nothing is ever uploaded.

What languages are supported?

Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, English, Japanese, Korean, and Spanish. You can switch manually in Settings or choose to follow your system language.

📋 关于本 App

《哈！爪爪》是一款纯本地、零联网的宠物健康记录工具。所有数据仅保存在您的手机中，无需注册账户，不上传任何信息。

您记录的体重、饮食、症状等信息，仅用于生成健康趋势和报告，帮助您更好地了解爱宠的健康状况。

❓ 常见问题

如何导出健康报告？

在「统计图表」页面向下滑动，点击「健康报告（PDF）」按钮，即可生成一份完整的健康报告。报告包含宠物档案、本周趋势、症状周报和费用明细，可直接分享给兽医。

如何添加多只宠物？

前往「设置」→「管理宠物」，点击「添加宠物」即可。添加后，首页和统计页顶部会出现宠物选择下拉菜单，点击即可切换查看不同宠物的数据。

数据会丢失吗？如何备份？

所有数据仅保存在您的手机本地。卸载 App 会导致数据永久删除，无法恢复。建议定期导出 PDF 报告作为备份，或手动截图保存重要信息。

App 需要网络权限吗？

不需要。本 App 不申请任何网络权限，所有操作均在本地完成，不会上传任何数据。

支持哪些语言？

简体中文、繁体中文、英文、日文、韩文、西班牙语。您可以在「设置」中手动切换，或选择跟随系统语言。
